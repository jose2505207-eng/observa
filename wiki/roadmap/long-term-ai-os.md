# Long-Term: Assistive AI OS

> The far horizon: OBSERVA as a permissioned assistive AI operating layer for blind and low-vision users. Direction only — not a commitment. See [[ai-os-expansion]].

## Where this goes
- Continuous on-device awareness ([[ambient-awareness]]) becomes the foundation for a broader assistant.
- Capabilities expand only via isolated, permissioned skills ([[mcp-skill-system]]): communication, productivity, navigation, translation, and more.
- The assistant mediates everyday digital + physical tasks through a voice-first interface ([[voice-command-layer]]).

## Invariants that never change
- The **offline safety core stays isolated** from all online capability ([[ADR-0001-offline-first]], [[ADR-0003-skill-system-boundaries]]).
- Cloud is always optional; airplane-mode usefulness is permanent ([[offline-first-design]]).
- Privacy-, accessibility-, and safety-first remain non-negotiable.

## Current Reality
None of this exists. This page exists to keep long-term direction coherent with today's architectural decisions.
